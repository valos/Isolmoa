New Game

Isolmoa

How to play

Each player places his piece, then:

For each turn of play, you must:

  • Moving your piece to a neighboring (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) position that contains a square but not the opponent's piece
  • Removing any square with no piece on it.

The goal of the game is to block the opponent by destroying all the squares which surround him before being blocked yourself.

Game options

You have choice between several sizes of board, from 4x4 to 8x8.

Players can be human or computer. Computer has 3 levels of AI (easy, normal, hard).
